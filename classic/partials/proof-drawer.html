Methodology

Fitting Procedure

Galaxy rotation curves are fit using the coherence field equation with only known baryonic mass distributions as input. No dark matter halo, no MOND interpolation function, no adjustable parameters beyond measured quantities.

The effective gravitational coupling geff(r) emerges directly from the coherence field τ(r), which solves:

d²τ/dr² + (2/r)(dτ/dr) = κ·τ·(1 - τ²)

Statistical Analysis

MCMC sampling with 4 independent chains, 100,000 steps each, Gelman-Rubin convergence diagnostic R̂ < 1.01 for all parameters. Burn-in: first 20,000 steps discarded.
